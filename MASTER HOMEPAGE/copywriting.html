In the Beginning was the...
Words came first. They should always come first in any campaign. They serve as the foundation for any idea or plan. They’re like spokes in a wheel, creating the tension necessary to hold things together and create movement. Choosing the right ones is a skill in itself, especially as language is an ever-evolving, living collection of textures and nuances.

Words have to work well with their graphical friends - and that’s where I come in. Words were in fact my first love, design coming later as a way of articulating.
