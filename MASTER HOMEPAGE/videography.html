STORYBOARDING
INFO
Creating storyboards to plot out the narrative of a video is always time well spent, but here it was an essential practice. Due to schedules, I wasn’t able to fly out to Seattle to direct the shoot in person with the crew of adventure film agency ‘Duct Tape then Beer’. I crafted these shot sketches to give them as much clarity as possible about what I was looking to capture.
